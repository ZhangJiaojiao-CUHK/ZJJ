Biography

I am a fourth-year PhD student in the Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Anthony Man-Cho So. I obtained my master degree in Automation from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2018 and my bachelor degree in Automation from Harbin Engineering University (HEU) in 2015. My research mainly focuses on optimization theory and the development of provably efficient algorithms for distributed optimization.

I am actively looking for postdoc/research positions in distributed optimization.
