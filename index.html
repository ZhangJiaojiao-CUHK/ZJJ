Biography

I am a third-year PhD student in the Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Anthony Man-Cho So. Prior to joining CUHK, I obtained my bachelor degree in Statistics from Chongqing University. My research mainly focuses on optimization theory and designing provably efficient optimization algorithms for machine learning.
