Biography

I am a third-year PhD student in the Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Anthony Man-Cho So. Prior to joining CUHK, I obtained my bachelor degree in Statistics from Chongqing University supervised by Prof. Lei Zhang on Transfer Learning. My interest lies in the intersections of Machine Learning and Optimization. Specially, I am currently interested in Distributionally Robust Optimization, Large Scale Optimization Algorithms and Reinforcement Learning.
